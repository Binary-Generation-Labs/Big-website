Passion led us to start binary generation labs

-Lead, Binary Generations Labs

As university students, one of our greatest regret was that, we were computer students with no prior experience building anything with the computer, we heard about programming languages, coding but had no experience.

On getting to the university, with high expectation, we were still doing the normal academics, calculation just like we did throughout secondary school. Then during this time which I had no way of escape, I saw some of our friends doing some crazy stuffs wth their laptops, all I knew to do was watch films and play games, I got interested in what they were doing, one was controlling a device from his laptop and another was writing somethings I later knew was called codes.

I then decided to get closer to this guys, because they were doing something no one knew how to do, on getting closer and starting to practice these things ourselves, our discussion one day went down to how they started and majority of them said they were introduced to it in high school, and then I knew why I didnâ€™t know it, so after the discussion, I decided that I will try our best to see that every young passionate tech enthusiast I come across I will help, having been doing that, I now decided with my friends to build an institute that can continue this act even if we are not available, so that gave birth to Binary Generation Labs, the name was chosen based on our decision to train students in bits.
